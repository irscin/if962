do or die mission to investigate what happened and save humanity  During this adventure  your only companion is a small robot named ASE  Together you will traverse the moon  explore abandoned facilities  gather clues and ultimately uncover the secrets and hidden agendas of those long gone  Will you save mankind or be forgotten in the dark abyss of Space REAL WORLD THEMESThe narrative
