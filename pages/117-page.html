Deliverance

A downloadable game for Windows

A game by @HitOrMissDev & @zombiedodgeking

Trapped in a mysterious library, you retrieve books to gain your freedom.

How to play - 

Look around with your Mouse and move forward by pressing the Q and E keys.

Moving:
In order to move successfully, you have to alternate between the Q and E keys. 
Press one first then release then press the other one. 

Successfully doing this will allow you to move forward, but failing to alternate the input will cause you to fall.
